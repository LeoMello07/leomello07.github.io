About Me

Hello there! I'm Leonardo Malheiros de Mello, a 22-year-old soon-to-be 23 from the vibrant land of Brazil. At my core, I'm a programmer, and this passion ignited in me at a young age. I remember the excitement of pressing F12 as a kid, gazing at the screen filled with a mesmerizing array of colorful words – even though their meaning eluded me at the time. It was a delightful mystery I couldn't resist. Fast forward to when I turned 17, I embarked on a journey through college, diving headfirst into the world of Software Engineering. Suddenly, those once enigmatic, sparkling letters on the computer screen began to form coherent patterns and meanings. It was as if a veil had been lifted, and I was deciphering a language that had always captivated me. Today, I proudly stand as a mobile developer specializing in crafting iOS applications. The evolution of my passion, from that wide-eyed fascination with luminous code to now creating functional and elegant applications, has been an exhilarating adventure. I can't wait to see where my programming journey takes me next!
